About Us

Since 2005, Automaniac Driving School has led the way in driving instruction. Automaniac Driving School has revolutionized and raised the bar for driving instruction in the nation thanks to its top-notch technology and training program.

We go the Extra mile

At Automaniac Driving School, we employ a cutting-edge curriculum that combines the best methods now used in India with our extensive experience providing driving instruction for over 50 years. Our curriculum covers the three essential components of driving: identification, judgement, and execution.
